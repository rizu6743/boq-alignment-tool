Pre-check before loading BOQs

Confirm these points before the tool builds the mapping tables.

1. Are both BOQs generally in the same bill/element alignment?
2. Are additional CCS items already incorporated in the Client BOQ as blank/unpriced rows?
3. If not incorporated, are CCS additional items clearly placed in a separate sheet or at the end of the relevant bill?
Stop: Arrange the BOQs properly first. Additional items must either be inserted in Client BOQ as blank rows, clearly located in a separate CCS sheet, or clearly placed at the end of the relevant bill.
