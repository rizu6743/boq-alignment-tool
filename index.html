Pre-check Before Mapping

The tool will continue only if the BOQs are arranged correctly for safe rate transfer.

1. Confirm both BOQs are in the same BOQ element alignment?
2. Are additional CCS items already incorporated into the Client BOQ?
3. If not incorporated, are the CCS additional items kept in a separate sheet or at the last section of the main bill?
Stop: Arrange the BOQs properly first. If additional items are mixed between existing bill items, incorporate blank unpriced lines into the Client BOQ, then re-upload.
