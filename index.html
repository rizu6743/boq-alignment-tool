Pre-check Before BOQ Processing

This tool must not proceed blindly. Confirm the BOQ arrangement first.

1. Are both BOQs aligned by BOQ elements / bill sequence?
2. Are CCS additional items already incorporated in the Client BOQ?
3. If not incorporated, are CCS additional items clearly arranged in a separate sheet or at the end of the respective bill/section?
Do not continue. Arrange the BOQs first: align bill sequence, keep additional CCS items clearly separated or place blank unpriced rows in Client BOQ where the items belong, then upload again.
