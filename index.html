BOQ Alignment & CCS Rate Transfer Tool

Upload the client BOQ and the Candy CCS exported BOQ. The tool keeps the client BOQ format as the base, transfers rates and totals from CCS, checks alignment, handles text rows such as “Included / Excluded / By Client”, and exports a priced client workbook plus an audit log.

1. Keep orderClient and CCS BOQs must follow the same bill/item order.
2. Map columnsSelect item, description, unit, quantity, rate and amount columns.
3. Confirm totalEnter the CCS expected bill total before transfer.
4. ExportDownload the priced client BOQ without changing its format.

Important instructions

  • Do not change the bill/item order in either BOQ.
  • Ignore main summaries and sub-summaries where possible; process bill/item sheets only.
  • If CCS has additional items between existing bill items, best practice is to insert the same blank lines in the client BOQ first, without rates, so both BOQs align clearly.
  • For additional items, choose whether to add them under the respective sheet, collect them in a separate sheet, or leave them for manual review.
  • The client BOQ is normally unpriced. The CCS BOQ must contain rate and amount.
